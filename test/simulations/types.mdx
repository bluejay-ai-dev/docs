Bluejay supports multiple ways to test an agent depending on the channel, customer behavior, and system architecture you need to evaluate.

## common simulation types

- Voice simulations for phone-based customer journeys
- Text simulations for messaging and webhook-based interactions
- Regression simulations for validating changes across known scenarios
- Scenario-specific simulations for stress cases, compliance checks, or edge conditions

## how it is used

Teams use simulation types to choose the right testing strategy for each stage of development, from early prototyping to launch readiness and post-change verification.
