Digital Humans are synthetic customers that interact with your agent during Bluejay evaluations. They make it possible to test realistic behavior before your product reaches production users.

## how it is used

You use Digital Humans to represent customer goals, tone, language, and edge-case behavior inside simulations. They help your team pressure-test agents across many different conversation patterns.
