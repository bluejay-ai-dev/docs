## Setting up

Bluejay is the end-to-end testing platform for your AI voice agents. 
Begin by creating an account on [Bluejay](https://app.getbluejay.ai).
After signing up, generate an API key from the [API Keys page](https://app.getbluejay.ai/api-keys).
With the Bluejay API, trigger our simulation and observability tools programmatically.
