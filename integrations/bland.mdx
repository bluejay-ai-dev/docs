This guide will walk you through how to connect your Bland phone numbers to Bluejay's observability software. You can select Bland as your Voice AI Provider when in the Advanced settings tab of an existing agent.

{/* <video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/retell-integration.mp4"></video> */}


### Quick Prerequisites

- **Bland Phone Number**
- **Bland Webhook Secret**
- **A Bluejay Account**

### Setting Up the Integration

### 1. Configure Organization Settings

1. Log into your **Bluejay Dashboard**
2. Click on your **Organization** button in the bottom left corner
3. Select the **Integrations** option 


![Navigate to Integrations](/images/integrations.png)

### 2. Adding Your Bland Key

1. Locate the **Bland Webhook Secret** input
2. Generate your Bland Webhook Signing Secret in the "General" tab under your organization's settings
3. Enter your Bland Webhook secret into the field 
3. Click **Save Webhook Key**

![Save API Key](/images/enter-vapi.png)

### 3. Configuring Your Agent in Bland

1. **Copy the Webhook URL given** in the Webhook configuration dropdown 
2. Navigate to your Bland Phone Numbers configutation screen and scroll down to the **Advanced** dropdown
3. **Paste the Webhook URL into the Webhook URL field**
4. Click **Save Inbound Call Changes**


<video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/configure-vapi-agent.mov"></video>

### 4. Configuring Your Agent in Bluejay

1. Navigate to your desired Agents **Settings** tab
2. Scroll down and click the **Advanced Settings** dropdown
3. Select **Bland as your Voice AI Provider** 
4. Copy and paste your Bland phone number into the **Phone Number** field (include the + icon and area code)
5. Click **Save Changes**
