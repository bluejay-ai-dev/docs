This guide walks you through how to connect your Retell voice agents to Bluejay's observability software. Once connected, every Retell call streams into Bluejay so you can monitor live conversations, evaluate transcripts against your custom metrics, and trigger alerts in real time. Retell becomes available as a Voice AI Provider on the Connection Settings tab of any existing agent.

<video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/retell-walk-through.mp4"></video>


### Prerequisites

- A Retell API Key
- Your Retell Agent ID
- A Bluejay account

### 1. Configure Organization Settings

Open the Bluejay integrations area where your Retell API key will be stored.

1. Log into your **Bluejay Dashboard**.
2. Click on **Settings** in the bottom left corner.
3. Select the **Integrations** option.

![Bluejay home dashboard](/images/retell-bluejay-home.gif)

### 2. Adding Your Retell Key

Connect Bluejay to your Retell account so your agents sync over and call events start flowing in.

1. Select the **Retell** tab in the Integrations panel.
2. Paste your Retell API key into the **API Key** field. The key must include the blue **Webhook** tag in Retell.
3. Click **Save** to store the key.
4. Click **Sync Agents from Retell** to import your Retell agents into Bluejay.

![Select the Retell integration](/images/retell-integrations-tab.gif)

### 3. Configuring Your Agent in Retell

Point your Retell agent at Bluejay so every call delivers events to your account.

1. In Retell, open your Agent's dashboard and click the **Webhook Settings** tab.
2. Paste this webhook URL into the **Agent Level Webhook URL** field:

```
https://api.getbluejay.ai/v1/integrations/retell
```

![Retell integration panel in Bluejay](/images/retell-api-key-sync.gif)

### 4. Configuring Your Agent in Bluejay

Link a Bluejay agent to the Retell agent it should mirror.

1. In Bluejay, open your Agent's **Settings** page.
2. Open the **Connection Settings** tab.
3. Select **Retell** as your Voice AI Provider.
4. Enter your Retell Agent ID in the **Match Agent ID** field.
5. Click **Save Changes**.

![Configure Retell in Connection Settings](/images/retell-connection-settings.gif)

### Quick Sync from the Plus Menu

For a faster path, you can sync your Retell agents directly from the quick add menu inside Bluejay.

1. Click the **+** button in the top left corner.
2. Select **Retell**.
3. Paste your Retell API key.
4. Click **Save and Sync** to import your Retell agents into Bluejay.

![Sync Retell from the Bluejay quick add menu](/images/retell-quick-sync.gif)
