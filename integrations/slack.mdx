Connect Slack to receive alarm alerts in a channel. When an alarm triggers or recovers, Bluejay sends a message to the Slack channel you choose. Connect Slack in Organization Settings, then select the channel when creating or editing an alarm.

### Quick Prerequisites

- **A Bluejay account**
- **A Slack workspace** where you can install the Bluejay app (admin or permission to add apps)

### Setting Up the Integration

### 1. Go to Integrations

1. Log into your **Bluejay Dashboard**
2. Click your **Organization** in the bottom left corner
3. Select **Integrations**
4. Open the **Slack** tab
5. Click **Connect** to start the Slack authorization

![Connect to Slack](/images/integrations-slack-connect.png)

### 2. Approve Bluejay in Slack

1. After clicking **Connect**, you are redirected to Slack
2. Review the requested permissions (channels and sending messages)
3. Click **Allow** (or "Approve for Bluejay") to authorize Bluejay for your workspace

![Approve Bluejay in Slack](/images/integrations-slack-approve.png)

### 3. Attach Slack to an alarm

1. Go to **Monitor → Alarms**
2. Create a new alarm or edit an existing one
3. In the notification section, turn on **Slack Notifications**
4. Choose a **Slack channel** from the dropdown (only populated when Slack is connected)
5. Save the alarm. When the alarm triggers or recovers, a message is sent to that channel

![Slack Notifications in alarm dialog](/images/integrations-slack-alarm-dialog.png)

### Disconnect Slack

To disconnect Slack from your organization, go to **Settings → Integrations**, open the **Slack** tab, and click **Disconnect**.
