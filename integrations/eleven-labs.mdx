This guide will walk you through how to connect your 11 Labs agents to Bluejay's observability software. You can select 11 Labs as your Voice AI Provider when in the Advanced settings tab of an existing agent.

{/* <video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/retell-integration.mp4"></video> */}


### Quick Prerequisites

- **11 Labs Webhook Secret**
- **A Bluejay Account**
- **Webhook URL:** `https://api.getbluejay.ai/v1/integrations/11labs`


### Setting Up the Integration

### 1. Configuring Your Agent in 11 Labs

1. Log into your 11 Labs account and go to **Settings**
2. Click on the **Select Webhook** button then click **Create Webhook**
3. Enter a name and then **Paste the Webhook URL into the Callback URL field**
4. Click **Create** and **Copy** the Secret Given 
5. Navigate to your 11 Labs Agent's dashboard and Click into the **Security Tab**
6. Select the Webhook you just created for your Agent and **Save Changes**

<video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="/videos/configure-vapi-agent.mov"></video>

### 2. Configure Organization Settings

1. Log into your **Bluejay Dashboard**
2. Click on your **Organization** button in the bottom left corner
3. Select the **Integrations** option 


![Navigate to Integrations](/images/integrations.png)

### 3. Adding Your 11 Labs Key

1. Locate the **11 Labs Webhook Secret** input
2. Paste the **Webhook Secret** given when configuring the Webhook in 11 Labs
3. Click **Save Webhook Key**

![Save API Key](/images/enter-vapi.png)





### 4. Configuring Your Agent in Bluejay

1. Navigate to your desired Agents **Settings** tab
2. Scroll down and click the **Advanced Settings** dropdown
3. Select **11 Labs as your Voice AI Provider** 
4. Copy and paste your 11 Labs Agent ID into the **Provider ID** field
5. Click **Save Changes**
